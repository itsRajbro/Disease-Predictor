About MediScan AI

Detection.

MediScan AI uses convolutional neural networks trained on open medical imaging datasets to provide fast, accessible disease screening for research and educational purposes.

Each model was trained using TensorFlow with data augmentation and dropout regularization to improve generalization across diverse image sources.
